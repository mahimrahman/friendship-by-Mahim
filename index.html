Types of Beautiful Friendships

Each friendship is unique, special, and brings its own magic to our lives

👶

Childhood Friends

These are the friends who have been with you since you were young. They know your history, your family, and have shared many formative experiences with you.

🎈 🧸 🎂
❤️

School Friends

These are people you have met and formed friendships with through attending school together. These friendships may be based on shared classes, extracurricular activities, or simply spending time together during school hours.

📚 🎓 ✏️
🌐

Online Friends

These are friends you have met online, through social media, forums, or gaming. You may share common interests or hobbies, and you communicate through messaging, video calls, or other online platforms.

💻 📱 🎮
💼

Work Friends

These are people you have a friendly relationship with at work. You may enjoy each other's company during breaks or after work, but your relationship is primarily based on your shared work environment.

☕ 💻 🎯
✈️

Travel Friends

These are friends you have met while traveling, either on a vacation or a long-term trip. You may have shared experiences or adventures together, and you have formed a close bond as a result.

🌍 📸 🎒
🌟

Friends Who Become Family

These are special friendships where your friends have become so close to you that they are like family members. They are people who have been there for you through thick and thin, have supported you, and have seen you at your best and worst.

⭐ 💎 🏆

Our Friendship Journey

A story of hearts that found each other and created something beautiful

🌱

🎭 The First Meeting - 2019

Year: 2019, Country: Malaysia, City: Cyberjaya. A bunch of people came across the world in an unknown city to study at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology. They were staying at University's off-campus dorm and were randomly living their life. No one knows no one. Everyone is just trying to meet random people, get to know each other and become friends.

🌿

🏠 Building Bonds - Same Neighborhood

When they got back from semester break, they moved to a different location and, as planned, everyone rented a house in the same condominium neighbourhood. All the close friends living in the same area, some in the same apartment, seem like a nice and decent idea. However, it did not last long when the covid 19 pandemic struck, effectively shutting everything down.

🌊

🚗 Adventures Together - Road Trip

One summer, the group decided to go on a road trip across the state. They packed their bags, filled their car with snacks and started their journey. Along the way, they faced many challenges but overcame them together. They sang songs, told stories, and enjoyed each other's company. They realized how lucky they were to have each other and how their friendship had become a family.

❤️

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Forever Family - Present

Years went by, and they all grew older. Some had families, but they continued to make time for each other. They continued to share their lives, and their friendship remained as strong as ever. They had built a bond that would last a lifetime, and they were grateful for the love and support that they had received from each other. They knew that their friendship had become a family, an irreplaceable family!
